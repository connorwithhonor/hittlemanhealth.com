The Calling

Why People Choose to Coach

When something changes your life, you want to share it with others.

You've Lived It

You've experienced the transformation yourself. You understand the struggle, the breakthroughs, and the joy of reclaiming your health. That personal journey makes you uniquely qualified to guide others.

You Believe in the Science

This isn't a fad diet or quick fix. It's a metabolic health approach backed by evidence. When you understand why it works, you want to help others experience it too.

You See People Struggling

Friends, family, coworkers—you see them trying everything and feeling defeated. You know there's a better way, and you want to be the person who helps them find it.

You Want Purpose

There's something deeply fulfilling about helping someone regain their confidence, energy, and hope. Coaching gives you the chance to make a real difference in people's lives.

Is This You?

Who Becomes a Coach

Coaching isn't about sales—it's about service. Here's who thrives in this role.

You Might Be Called to Coach If...

  • You've experienced the program and believe in it deeply
  • People naturally come to you for advice and support
  • You're passionate about health and helping others succeed
  • You're coachable and open to learning
  • You value community and collaboration
  • You want to be part of something bigger than yourself
  • You're willing to grow alongside the people you help

This Probably Isn't for You If...

  • You're not genuinely passionate about health
  • You're looking for something easy or instant
  • You're not comfortable supporting others through challenges
  • You prefer to work alone rather than as part of a team

For Healthcare Providers

Partner With Us

Help others in your community reach their health goals, no medical license required—and help bridge the gap between visits with medical professionals through lifestyle support for your patients.

You don't have to be a healthcare practitioner to make a difference. If you have a passion for helping others achieve their health goals—whether you're a teacher, parent, fitness enthusiast, community leader, or simply someone who cares—you can learn to coach and support others using these proven methods. You don't need to be a medical expert to do what is taught here. All you need is a desire to help, a willingness to learn, and a commitment to walk alongside others on their health journey.

I'm currently looking to collaborate with physicians, nurse practitioners, chiropractors, functional providers, therapists, and allied health professionals who want additional lifestyle support resources for their patients while they remain under medical care.

I partner with healthcare professionals to help bridge the gap between visits by providing patients with structured nutrition education, habit coaching, accountability, and ongoing support for conditions related to metabolic dysfunction—including insulin resistance, prediabetes, Type 2 diabetes, weight-related health concerns, and cardiometabolic risk factors.

If you're open to collaboration, referrals, or simply connecting to learn more about how we can support patients together, I'd love to connect.
